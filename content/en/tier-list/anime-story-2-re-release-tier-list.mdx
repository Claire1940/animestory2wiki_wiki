<Section icon={Shield} title="The Leader System & Tank Meta">

Update 0.5 introduced the Leader Category, which has completely shifted the **anime story 2 re release tier list**. Leaders only provide their bonuses if placed in **Slot 1** of your team. This forces players to choose between raw damage buffs, health increases, or utility like critical hit chance.

**Leader Skill Breakdown:**
- **Asta (Devil)**: Shortens all game damage by 15% (Universal Buff).
- **Fuegoleona**: Brawler allies gain 10% damage and doubles all burn/flame damage.
- **Yuno (Ascended)**: Rival allies gain 20% Max HP.
- **Yami**: Captain allies gain 15% critical chance.

<Callout type="info" title="Tank Spotlight">
**Yami** is currently the only unit that can survive a fatal hit with 1 HP and gain immunity for a turn. This makes him the best "stalling" unit for difficult boss encounters.
</Callout>
