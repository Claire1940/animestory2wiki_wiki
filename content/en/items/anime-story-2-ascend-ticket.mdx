There are several reliable methods to acquire **Ascend Tickets** in Anime Story 2, ranging from direct purchases to completing in-game activities. Understanding these sources will help you plan your farming strategy effectively.

### Method 1: The Coin Shop
The Coin Shop is arguably the most direct way to get your hands on **Ascend Tickets**. If you've been diligently grinding for coins, this is where you can convert your hard-earned currency into valuable ascension materials.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Shop">
    From the game's lobby, navigate to the main menu and select the 'Shop' option.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate Upgrade Items">
    Within the shop, look for the 'Upgrade Items' section. This category typically houses all materials needed for character progression.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Purchase Ascend Tickets">
    Find the **Ascend Ticket** item and purchase as many as your coin balance allows. Prices may vary based on quantity or special offers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Coin Management">
While the Coin Shop offers a direct route, be mindful of your coin reserves. Ascend Tickets can be expensive, and you'll need coins for other essential upgrades and summons as well. Balance your spending to avoid running out of currency for critical needs.
</Callout>

### Method 2: Quests and Achievements
Another consistent way to earn **Ascend Tickets** is by completing various in-game quests and achievements. These often reward you with tickets as you progress through the game's story or fulfill specific tasks.

| Quest Type | Example Objective | Reward Frequency | Notes |

| **Daily Quests** | Defeat 50 enemies, Complete 3 dungeons | Low (1-2 tickets) | Resets daily, consistent small gains |
| **Weekly Quests** | Clear a specific raid, Ascend 1 unit | Medium (3-5 tickets) | Resets weekly, good for mid-term goals |
| **Story Quests** | Complete Chapter X, Defeat Boss Y | High (5-10 tickets) | One-time rewards, significant boosts |
| **Event Quests** | Participate in limited-time events | Varies (often high) | Check event schedules, time-sensitive |

<Callout type="info">

  <Step num="2" title="Select Your Unit">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate Ascension">
