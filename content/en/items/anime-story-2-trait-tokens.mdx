<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Farming Trait Tokens">
Farming Trait Tokens efficiently requires a strategic approach, combining quick wins with consistent long-term efforts. Follow these steps to maximize your token acquisition.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Redeem Active Codes">
    Always start by checking for and redeeming any active Anime Story 2 codes. Codes are frequently released by developers and can grant a significant number of Trait Tokens instantly. Websites like PCGamesN often update their code lists. For example, codes like `5k`, `asta`, and `naturetower` have provided hundreds of tokens in the past.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Complete Daily and Weekly Quests">
    These quests are your most reliable and consistent source of Trait Tokens. Daily quests refresh every 24 hours, while weekly quests reset once a week. They often involve simple tasks like clearing stages, defeating specific enemies, or using certain units.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Conquer the Tower">
    The Tower offers a substantial amount of Trait Tokens upon its weekly reset. You typically earn 5 Trait Tokens for every 5 floors cleared. Progressing as high as possible in the Tower each week can net you hundreds of tokens.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Participate in Raids">
    While not the primary source, raids offer a chance to drop Trait Tokens, especially when farming for shiny units. Each raid run has a small chance (e.g., 1%) to yield 1-2 tokens. This is a bonus while pursuing other raid-specific rewards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Check the Coin Shop Regularly">
    The Coin Shop often has Trait Tokens available for purchase using in-game currency. These purchases might be limited daily or weekly, so make it a habit to check the shop and buy tokens if you have surplus coins.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Progress Through the Battle Pass">
    If you've invested in the Battle Pass, ensure you're completing tasks to advance its tiers. The Battle Pass often includes Trait Tokens as tiered rewards, providing a bulk sum as you play through the season.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
Remember that some codes expire quickly. Bookmark reliable sources for codes and check them frequently to avoid missing out on free tokens.
</Callout>
