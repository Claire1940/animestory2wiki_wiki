<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Late-Game Mastery: The 30% Stat Tier">

Reaching the end-game in 2026 requires a deep understanding of the 30% stat tier relics. These are the most powerful singular items in the game, providing massive boosts that allow units to solo high-difficulty content. The Flame Sword, Baseball Bat, and Demon Ring represent the pinnacle of pure stat optimization. 

The Flame Sword is often considered the best-in-slot for units that rely on critical hits to trigger passive abilities. Conversely, the Baseball Bat is the go-to for raw damage dealers who already have high natural crit rates. The Demon Ring is essential for "tanking" units that need to survive the heavy hits found in World 5 and beyond.

| End-Game Relic | Specific Stat | Shard Type | Impact Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Flame Sword** | +30% Crit Chance | 500 Legendary Shards | Massive DPS Spike |
| **Baseball Bat** | +30% Damage | 500 Mythic Shards | High Burst Potential |
| **Demon Ring** | +30% Health | 500 Dark Shards | Maximum Tanking |

<Callout type="success" title="Meta Strategy">
If your unit already has a built-in crit chance of 50% or higher from their kit or potential, the Baseball Bat (+30% Damage) will almost always provide a higher average DPS increase than the Flame Sword. Always calculate your total crit before choosing your relic.
</Callout>

Hybrid relics like Coal and Candy offer a more nuanced approach. Coal is unique because it provides both health and damage reduction, making it the most effective survival tool in the game. Candy, on the other hand, is the ultimate "all-rounder" for DPS units, combining 20% damage with 10% crit chance. This is often preferred over a pure 30% damage relic if the unit needs just a little more crit consistency to perform optimally.

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Farm Shards Efficiently">

Farming the materials for **anime story 2 relics** requires a focused approach. Shards are color-coded and tied to specific worlds. To maximize your efficiency, you should identify which relic you need next and target the corresponding world's Stage 7. Stage 7 is the only consistent source of high-volume shard drops.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify the Required Color">
    Check the crafting menu in the lobby to see which shard color is needed for your desired relic (e.g., Green for Dragon Ball, Orange for Kunai).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Progress to Stage 7">
    Enter the world associated with that shard color and progress until you reach Stage 7. This stage typically features a boss or a higher density of elite mobs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Optimize Clear Speed">
    Equip your best AoE units to clear the stage as quickly as possible. Since shard drops are RNG-dependent, the number of runs per hour is your most important metric.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize Potions and Boosts">
    If you have Drop Rate potions or game passes, Stage 7 is the best place to activate them to double your shard acquisition rate.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="World Mapping">
Each world in Anime Story 2 drops a different shard color. World 1 typically drops Green, World 2 drops Orange/Blue, and later worlds drop the specialized Red, Purple, and Legendary shards required for the top-tier relics.
</Callout>
