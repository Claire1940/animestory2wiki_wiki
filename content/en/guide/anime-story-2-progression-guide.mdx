<Section icon={Swords} title="Unit Upgrades, Ascension, and Relic Crafting">

To push through the higher difficulty levels of each world, you must utilize the game's various upgrade systems. Simply leveling up your units will eventually hit a ceiling, requiring you to invest in Ascension, Evolution, and Relics.

Ascension is the process of raising a unit's maximum level cap. For instance, a standard unit like Goku initially caps at level 50. By sacrificing duplicate copies of the same unit, you can ascend them up to Tier 5, which raises their maximum level cap to 300. Notably, Secret and pre-ascended units bypass this requirement entirely, starting with a maximum level cap of 300 automatically.

| Upgrade Type | Upgrade Method | Main Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ascension** | Sacrifice duplicate units | Unlocks level cap up to **level 300** |
| **Evolution** | Use specific evolution items | Enhances skills and **base stats** |
| **Relic Crafting** | Use shards from final bosses | Grants massive **passive damage boosts** |
| **Trait Rerolling** | Spend trait tokens on traits | Yields powerful **unique passives** |

Beyond leveling, optimizing your units' stats and traits is crucial. The Worthiness system, which builds up as you defeat enemies, increases your chances of rolling higher-tier stats using stat chips. Additionally, you should take advantage of the Shiny Transfer system. Shiny units deal significantly more damage than their standard counterparts, and transferring this status to your core meta units is key to clearing late-game content.

Relics represent another major power spike. For any dedicated DPS unit, the Baseball Bat relic is the absolute best-in-slot choice, granting a massive 50% damage increase. Crafting relics requires specific colored shards, which are dropped exclusively by the final boss NPC of each world.

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Be careful when selecting units to sacrifice for Ascension. Ensure you do not accidentally consume shiny variants, as their bonus damage is vital for late-game progression.
</Callout>

Raids are essential for acquiring top-tier meta units like Aizen, Itachi, and Pain. Clearing Act 6 of these raids gives you a chance to secure their shiny variants, which are highly sought after for their superior scaling. The tokens earned from these raids can be spent in the Raid Shop, which resets daily and offers valuable items such as luck potions, trait tokens, and stat chips.

Challenges are currently the only reliable way to farm the Stars required for unit evolution, short of purchasing them for high prices in the coin shop. These challenges reset every half hour and often feature difficult debuffs, such as doubling the enemies' base stats. 

The Slime Apocalypse event presents a unique challenge where your team's health does not regenerate between the three boss fights. Defeating the final boss, Rimuru, rewards you with Slime Tokens. These tokens can be gambled on the event banner for a chance to pull Rimuru, stat cubes, mythic orbs, and trade tokens.
