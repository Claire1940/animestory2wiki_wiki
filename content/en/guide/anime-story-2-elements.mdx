<Section icon={Swords} title="Best Units for Each Element">

Choosing the right unit for each of the **anime story 2 elements** can make or break your endgame progression. While some units excel in raw damage, others provide invaluable utility such as stuns, heals, or revives. Let us break down the top-performing units across all elemental categories.

### Fire and Water Elements
Fire boasts incredible damage-over-time capabilities. **Leona** remains the premier Fire-type unit, while **Killer Rebirth** (Kira) offers a highly unique playstyle. Kira applies bomb marks to single targets; at three marks, the target detonates, removing a random passive. Additionally, when Kira's health falls below 30%, he reflects all incoming damage back to the attacker once per battle, making him a potential meta pick for Tower strategies.

For the Water element, **Remi** stands unchallenged as the top choice. Her consistent damage and elemental synergy make her a staple for any team facing Fire-dominated stages.

### Nature and Spark Elements
The Nature element has received a massive upgrade with the introduction of **Golden Wind** (Giorno). Giorno is currently the strongest Nature unit in the game. He plants life seeds on enemies, dealing 1.35x damage and healing the weakest ally. His passive, *Return to Zero*, forces enemies to skip their turn, while *Bring to Life* allows him to revive a fallen ally with full damage reflection for one turn.

In the Spark category, **Tengen** and **Sound Breather** dominate [web]. Tengen is highly sought after for Tower runs due to his massive damage output, especially when paired with Dark-type support units. **Guitarist** (Kars) is another fantastic Spark option, boosting Jojo allies' damage by up to 100% based on the number of Jojo units on the field.

### Light, Dark, and Neutral Elements
The Light and Dark elements feature some of the most powerful offensive units. **Majestic Vampire** (Dio) is a dominant Light/Dark competitor, gaining stacking crit chance and crit damage. Every three turns, he freezes time, allowing him to take an extra turn. **Brandon** (Dio) acts as a high-utility Blood Puppeteer, applying bleed and healing himself. For pure Dark, **Asta** (Devil) is highly favored due to his complete immunity to status effects, while **Aizen** provides incredible crowd control with his stuns.

In the Neutral category, **Guts** remains the gold standard. However, **Greatest Maid** is an exceptional support unit, boosting all neutral units' damage by 30% and increasing drop rates by 10% [web]. **Cursed Robot** (WOU) is also highly viable, summoning tanky clones that taunt enemies and explode upon death [web].

| **Fire** | Killer Rebirth | Bomb marks, damage reflection, passive removal | Tower Specialist |
| **Water** | Remi | Excellent elemental scaling, high survival | Wave Clearer |
| **Nature** | Golden Wind | Revive, heal, turn-skipping passive | Hybrid Support/DPS |
| **Spark** | Guitarist | Buffs Jojo allies up to 100% damage | Team Buffer |
| **Dark** | Asta (Devil) | Status immunity, meta-proof scaling | Boss Killer |
| **Dark** | Aizen | Massive 900k first-hit damage, reliable stuns | Crowd Control |
| **Neutral** | Greatest Maid | 10% drop rate buff, 30% Neutral damage buff | Utility Support |
| **Neutral** | Cursed Robot | Summons exploding tank clones to taunt | Tank/DPS Hybrid |
