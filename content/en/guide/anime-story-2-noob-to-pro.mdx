<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Trait Optimization: Monarch vs. Ethereal">

In Anime Story 2, your unit's trait can be more important than its level. When pushing from noob to pro status, you will encounter two top-tier traits: **Monarch** and **Ethereal**. While both are powerful, they serve different roles in your team composition.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Targeting Monarch">
    Monarch provides a massive boost to both HP and Damage. It is the best trait for units that need to survive multiple hits while still dealing significant AoE damage, such as Tangen or Slime Demon.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Ethereal Alternative">
    Ethereal focuses purely on damage output. If you have a unit like Asta who already has high survivability through passives, Ethereal can help push his DPS to the absolute limit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Pity Management">
    Save your rerolls for the **Monarch Pity** (usually around 600-700 rolls). Do not waste rolls on secondary units until your main DPS has a Rainbow-tier trait.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Stat Note">
To guarantee Triple-S (SSS) stats, you typically need to spend approximately 6,500 stat chips. Prioritize Attack and Crit Damage over Health for your main damage dealers.
</Callout>
