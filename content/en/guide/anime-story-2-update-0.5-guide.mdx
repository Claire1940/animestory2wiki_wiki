<Section icon={Target} title="Nature Tower Walkthrough">

The Nature Tower is a cornerstone of the **anime story 2 update 0.5 guide**. This new mode challenges players to climb floors with increasing difficulty, rewarding those who can balance damage and survivability. Unlike standard worlds, the Tower requires specific strategies to overcome floor bosses.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build a Balanced Team">
    Ensure you have at least one dedicated tank (like Titan or Black Bull) and one healer (like Roku Rose). The enemies in later floors deal massive AoE damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Focus on Elemental Synergy">
    Use units that buff specific elements. For the Nature Tower, units like Tatsumaki (+30% Nature DMG) provide a massive edge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Manage Floor Milestones">
    Every 100 floors, you have a chance to get a limited unit drop. Focus on reaching these milestones first before attempting to "speed-run" the tower.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize Reroll Buffs">
    Every 250 floors, the tower loops and grants 100 bonus rerolls. Use these to refine the stats of your main carry unit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Difficulty Spike">
Be prepared for a massive difficulty jump around floor 200. If your Combat Power is below 150K, you may need to farm relics in previous worlds before pushing further.
</Callout>
