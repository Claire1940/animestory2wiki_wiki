Gems are the primary currency for acquiring new units through summons. Maximizing your gem income is paramount for expanding your roster and obtaining powerful characters.

### Early Game Gem Farming (New Players)

For new players, finding a reliable and efficient stage to AFK farm is key. The goal is to clear stages quickly and consistently to accumulate gems.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify Your Strongest AoE Unit">
    Early in the game, **Ace** is an excellent legendary unit with full AoE capabilities. If you acquire him, he will be your primary farmer. Even without specific traits, he performs well.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Find a Suitable Story Map">
    Test different story maps to find the highest difficulty you can consistently solo with Ace. For many new players, **Desert 2-3** or **Beach Harbor** on Nightmare difficulty are good starting points.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="AFK Farming">
    Once you identify a suitable map, set your team to AFK farm overnight or during periods you're away. This passive gem income is crucial for early progression.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Optimize Ace's Traits (Optional)">
    If you have access to trait rerolls, consider aiming for the **Berserk** trait on Ace to further enhance his farming efficiency.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
On Nightmare difficulty, a successful clear with Ace can yield around **270 gems**. Consistency is more important than pushing an overly difficult stage that results in failures.
</Callout>

### AFK Farming Setup

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Stable Connection">
    Ensure you have a stable internet connection to prevent disconnections during long AFK sessions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Optimal Stage Selection">
    Always choose the highest difficulty stage you can **consistently** clear without failure. A slightly lower reward with 100% success is better than higher rewards with frequent failures.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Minimize Interruptions">
    Disable any power-saving settings on your device that might cause the game to close or pause.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Check Periodically">
    Even with an optimized setup, it's good practice to check on your AFK farm periodically to ensure it's running smoothly.
