<Section icon={Swords} title="Optimizing Unit Traits and Stats">
The Trait system in *Anime Story 2* provides stat bonuses to your units, significantly enhancing their capabilities. Rerolling for optimal Traits is a key aspect of maximizing your units' power. Traits can boost Health, Damage, Critical Damage, and Critical Chance, all crucial for overcoming difficult challenges.

### Acquiring and Using Trait Tokens

Trait Tokens are essential for rerolling Traits. They are relatively easy to obtain through various in-game activities.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain Trait Tokens">
    Acquire **Trait Tokens** by completing stages, redeeming codes, or purchasing them from the in-game shop.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate to Upgrades">
    Access the "Upgrades" area using the Areas menu within the game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Access Trait Reroll">
    Locate the "Traits" section within the Upgrades menu.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reroll Traits">
    Use your **Trait Tokens** to reroll a unit's Trait, aiming for the most beneficial stat bonuses.
  </Step>
</Steps>
