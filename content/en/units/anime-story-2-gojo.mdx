<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimizing Stats and Traits for Gojo">

To make your **Anime Story 2 Gojo** truly effective, you must dive into the Stat and Trait systems. Stats are individual attributes that can be rerolled using chips, while Traits are passive abilities that provide massive percentage-based buffs. For a support unit like Gojo, you aren't just looking for raw attack power; you want a balance of speed and utility.

The "Worthiness" system is a critical mechanic here. As you achieve more takedowns, your worthiness increases, which in turn raises the probability of rolling higher-tier stats. Never spend your high-tier reroll chips until your worthiness is at its peak.

| Stat Type | Priority for Gojo | Optimal Grade |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Speed | Very High | S or Higher |
| Critical Chance | High | A+ or Higher |
| Health | Medium | B+ or Higher |
| Attack | Medium | B+ or Higher |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build Worthiness">
    Farm lower-level stages to quickly increase your takedown count and maximize your worthiness level before rerolling.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Trait Rolling">
    Use Trait Tokens to aim for **Ethereal or Monarch**. These are the top-tier traits that provide the most significant boosts in the 2026 update.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Shiny Transfer">
    If you obtain a shiny version of a lower-tier unit, use the Shiny Transfer system to move that status to Gojo, increasing his base damage and visual flair.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Reroll chips are scarce. Do not waste "Reroll All" chips on units that already have two or three perfect stats. Use single stat chips to fine-tune your Gojo build without risking your best attributes.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Advanced Progression: Ascension and Relics">

Once your **Anime Story 2 Gojo** reaches the level cap, you must utilize the Ascension system to continue growing. Ascension requires sacrificing duplicate units or using Ascension Tickets to increase the star rating of your character. Each star increases the level cap by 50, all the way up to Tier 5 (Level 300).

Furthermore, the Crafting station (though sometimes temperamental) allows you to create Relics. For any unit that contributes to damage or utility, the **Baseball Bat** is the undisputed king of relics in the current version.

| Relic Name | Crafting Requirement | Primary Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Baseball Bat | Brown Shards (Last World) | +50% Damage Output |
| Elixir | Green Shards | +25% Max HP |
| Speed Boots | Blue Shards | +15% Attack Speed |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Level to 50">
    Reach the initial level cap through story progression and XP potions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Ascend to Tier 5">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Equip Relics">
    Farm the final boss of the last world to gather Brown Shards for the Baseball Bat relic.
  </Step>
</Steps>
