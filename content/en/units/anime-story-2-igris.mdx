<Section icon={Target} title="Best Traits and Reroll Strategy">

Traits are passive modifiers applied to your units via the Trait NPC located in the lobby's Upgrade area. Rerolling traits requires Trait Tokens, which can be acquired through the Coin Shop, active codes, quests, and daily challenges. 

Because Shadow Knight is a hybrid unit, choosing the correct trait determines whether he functions as an unstoppable frontline tank or a devastating critical-strike carry.

| Trait Name | Roll Chance | Stat Buffs | Meta Viability |
|---|---|---|---|

| **Dead Eye** | 2.0% | +40% Critical Damage, +40% Critical Chance | Elite Offense (S) |

If you are a free-to-play player with limited Trait Tokens, stopping at **Berserk** or **Tank** is highly recommended. However, if you are preparing Shadow Knight for endgame raids or the Tower of Ascension, you should save your tokens to chase **Dead Eye** or the ultra-rare **Monarch** trait.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Optimal Relic Builds and Crafting">

Relics are powerful equipment items crafted in the Upgrade Area. Each unit can equip only one relic at a time. To craft relics, you must farm specific Shards from the Stage VII levels of various story worlds.

For the **anime story 2 igris** build, your relic choice should complement his high base damage and survival mechanics. Increasing his critical strike parameters or flat damage scaling yields the best combat results.

| Relic Name | Stat Effects | Crafting Requirements | Primary Source Stage |

| **Baseball Bat** | +30% Damage | x200 Brown Shard, ¥20,000,000 | Heroic City Stage VII |
| **Candy** | +20% DMG, +10% Crit | x150 Purple, x150 Brown Shard, ¥30,000,000 | Snow Village & Heroic City |
| **Katana** | +20% Damage | x150 Red Shard, ¥2,000,000 | Gold District Stage VII |

If you are in the early-to-mid game, the **Katana** provides a massive 20% damage boost for a relatively low crafting cost. For the absolute endgame, the **Flame Sword** or the hybrid **Candy** relic will allow Shadow Knight to land devastating critical hits consistently.
