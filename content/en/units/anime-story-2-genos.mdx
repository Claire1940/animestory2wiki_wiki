<Section icon={Target} title="How to Acquire and Apply Traits to Genos">
Acquiring and applying Traits in Anime Story 2 is a straightforward process, primarily revolving around **Trait Tokens**. These tokens are your currency for rerolling and assigning a random Trait to any of your units, including Genos.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain Trait Tokens">
    Trait Tokens are essential for interacting with the Trait system. They can be acquired through several in-game activities:
    - **Stage Clears**: Successfully completing game stages often rewards you with Trait Tokens.
    - **In-game Shop Purchases**: The shop periodically offers Trait Tokens for purchase using in-game currency.
    - **Code Redemptions**: Keep an eye out for active codes released by the developers, as these frequently include Trait Tokens as rewards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate to the Upgrades Area">
    Once you have Trait Tokens, you need to access the dedicated Traits section. Use the 'Areas' menu within the game to navigate to the 'Upgrades' area, and then select the 'Traits' option.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Apply Traits to Genos">
    In the Traits section, select Genos from your unit roster. You can then use your Trait Tokens to assign a single random Trait to him. This process can be repeated until you roll a desired Trait.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Rerolling Considerations">
Rerolling Traits is based on luck. While it's tempting to keep rerolling for the best possible Trait, remember that each attempt consumes a Trait Token. Plan your rerolls strategically, especially for your most important units like Genos.
</Callout>

</Section>
