Relics are vital equipment items crafted in the Upgrade Area of the lobby. Unlike basic stat adjustments, relics provide massive percentage-based boosts that scale directly with your unit's level. For a critical-heavy character like this one, choosing a relic that enhances either raw critical chance or overall damage output will yield the best results in high-level raids and challenges.

To craft these powerful items, you must farm specific Stage VII levels across the game's various worlds. Prioritizing these farming routes early in your progression ensures you do not hit a wall when transitioning into late-game content.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Best Traits and Reroll Strategy">

Traits are unique passive buffs applied to units by interacting with the Trait NPC located in the lobby's Upgrade Area. Each unit can hold exactly one trait at a time. Rerolling traits requires Trait Tokens, which are obtained via active codes, quests, the Coin Shop, and Daily Challenges. Because high-tier traits have extremely low roll rates, managing your token economy is crucial.

For this offensive powerhouse, traits that enhance critical strike chance, critical damage, and raw attack power are highly prioritized.
