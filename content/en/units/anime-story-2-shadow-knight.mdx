<Section icon={Shield} title="Anime Story 2 Shadow Knight Profile & Core Mechanics">

The **anime story 2 shadow knight** stands as one of the most versatile and resilient units in the entire game. Classified under the ultra-rare Secret category, this unit bridges the gap between an unyielding defensive frontline and a pressure-heavy damage dealer. While many players initially view it solely as a survival tank due to its massive base health pool, its bleed-inducing mechanics and mark-chaining capabilities allow it to function as a highly competent primary carry for both beginner and endgame teams.

Understanding the base statistics and passive behavior of the Shadow Knight is crucial before investing valuable resources like Trait Tokens and Ascend Tickets. At base level, the unit boasts exceptional survivability metrics that outclass almost all Mythic options.

| Metric | Value | Detail |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Unit Rarity | Secret | Extremely low summon rate from standard banners |
| Base HP | 1.05K | Top-tier starting health pool for frontline placement |
| Base DMG | 210 | Solid base scaling for physical and bleed damage |
| Core Role | Survival Tank | Designed to absorb fatal boss mechanics |
| Passive Ability | Shadow Shield | Grants 2 turns of total damage immunity on fatal hit |
| Combat Specialty | Bleed & Mark | Punishes marked targets and triggers extra attacks |

The defining feature of the Shadow Knight is its passive ability, Shadow Shield. When the unit takes damage that would normally defeat it, the shield triggers automatically, rendering the Shadow Knight completely immune to all incoming damage for the next two rounds of combat. This mechanical safety net allows your support and damage-dealing allies to clean up difficult encounters without fear of a sudden wipe. Furthermore, in starter configurations, the unit applies bleed marks at the start of battle, enabling rapid clearing of early story stages.

<Callout type="tip" title="Survival Strategy">
Place the Shadow Knight in your first team slot. This ensures it absorbs the brunt of single-target boss attacks, maximizing the value of its 2-turn damage immunity shield while keeping your backline DPS units safe.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimal Traits & Reroll Strategy">

Traits are passive modifiers applied to your units at the Trait NPC located in the Upgrade area of the main lobby. Rerolling these traits requires Trait Tokens, which can be acquired through daily quests, challenges, shop purchases, or active promotional codes. Because the Shadow Knight is a Secret unit, it deserves your highest-priority Trait Tokens.

Depending on whether you want to build your Shadow Knight as a pure, unkillable tank or a hybrid bleed carry, your target traits will differ. 

| Trait Name | Roll Chance | Buff Effects | Best Application | Reroll Recommendation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Monarch | 0.1% | +25% Crit, +50% Crit DMG, +75% HP, +75% DMG | Best All-Rounder | Stop immediately and lock |
| Solar | 2.0% | +33% Health, +33% Damage | Hybrid Bruiser | Keep unless chasing Monarch |
| Dead Eye | 2.0% | +40% Critical Damage, +40% Critical Chance | Offensive Carry | Keep for DPS-focused builds |
| Tank | 11.0% | +25% Health | Pure Frontline | Keep on pure defensive setups |
| Berserk | 11.0% | +25% Damage | Budget Carry | Keep early; reroll for Solar/Monarch |
| Cosmic | 11.0% | +15% Health, +15% Damage | Balanced Budget | Acceptable temporary placeholder |

If you are a free-to-play player with limited Trait Tokens, aiming for Tank or Berserk is a highly practical stopping point. These traits provide a clean 25% boost to either health or damage, allowing the Shadow Knight to perform its job exceptionally well in standard story worlds. For endgame players, saving tokens to chase Solar or the elusive Monarch trait is mandatory to survive high-tier raids and deep Tower runs.

<Callout type="warning" title="Token Management">
Do not spend your last Trait Tokens chasing a 0.1% Monarch roll on the Shadow Knight if your other four team members have completely empty or low-tier traits. A balanced team with Vigor III or Impair III traits outperforms a single Monarch unit supported by weak allies.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Progression & Relic Build Guide">

Optimizing the **anime story 2 shadow knight** requires a structured upgrade path. Players must transition the unit from a raw summon into a fully geared, ascended powerhouse by crafting compatible relics and farming specific world stages.

Follow these steps to maximize your unit's efficiency:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquisition and Leveling">
    Acquire the Shadow Knight through banners or by collecting 50 Secret Orbs from high-tier quests. Once obtained, immediately slot it into your active team and clear high-EXP story stages to raise its base level to the current cap.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Trait Alignment">
    Visit the Trait NPC in the Upgrade area. Spend your accumulated Trait Tokens to roll for at least a tier-three defensive or offensive trait. Aim for Tank, Berserk, or Solar to establish a strong baseline of performance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Relic Crafting">
    Farm the final stages of specific worlds to collect crafting shards. Bring these shards to the Upgrade Area to craft a relic that matches your build path. Equip the relic directly to your Shadow Knight to unlock massive secondary stats.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Ascension">
    Acquire Ascend Tickets from the Coin Shop or milestone quests. Use these tickets to ascend your Shadow Knight, raising its level cap and multiplying its overall base health and damage attributes for late-game content.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Relics are critical for min-maxing your unit's performance. Since you can only equip one relic per unit, choosing the correct recipe is vital.

| Relic Name | Shard Requirement | Source Location | Passive Buff | Build Path Role |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Elixir | x100 Blue Shard | Double Dungeon Stage VII | +10% Health | Early-game survival boost |
| Strawhat | x150 Yellow Shard | Beach Harbor Stage VII | +20% Health | Mid-game pure tank build |
| Katana | x150 Red Shard | Gold District Stage VII | +20% Damage | Mid-game hybrid carry build |
| Candy | x150 Purple, x150 Brown | Snow Village & Heroic City | +20% DMG, +10% Crit | Late-game optimal hybrid |
| Coal | x150 Purple, x150 Brown | Snow Village & Heroic City | +20% HP, +10% DMG Reduction | Late-game ultimate tank |

For a pure defensive build, the Coal relic paired with a Tank trait makes the Shadow Knight almost impossible to take down. Conversely, equipping the Candy relic alongside a Solar or Monarch trait turns the unit into a devastating front-line bruiser that can clear waves single-handedly.

<Callout type="info" title="Farming Tip">
When farming shards for late-game relics like Coal or Candy, make sure to consume a Drops Potion or Luck Potion purchased from the Coin Shop. This significantly increases your shard yield per run, saving hours of repetitive grinding.
</Callout>
