The Rimuru Portal Event consists of three distinct waves, each increasing in difficulty. Understanding the dynamics of each wave is crucial for a successful solo clear in **Anime Story 2**.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Wave 1: Initial Engagement">
    The first wave is generally straightforward. Deploy your **Broly** early to start clearing the initial enemies. Focus on getting him to a strong position where he can cover as much of the lane as possible. This wave serves as a warm-up and allows you to build up some initial resources.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Wave 2: Increasing Pressure">

Position your crowd control units to slow down or stun incoming threats, giving Broly more time to eliminate them. Monitor your unit's health and be ready to activate any available abilities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Wave 3: The Final Challenge">
    The third wave is the most difficult, often featuring stronger elite enemies or a mini-boss. This wave requires precise timing and ability usage. Consistent damage output is essential to clear this wave within the time limit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Don't Underestimate Wave 3">
