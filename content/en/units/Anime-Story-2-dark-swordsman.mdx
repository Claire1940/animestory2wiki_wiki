<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimal Traits for Dark Swordsman">
Choosing the right traits can significantly amplify the Dark Swordsman's effectiveness. Traits are passive buffs applied to units that can be rerolled using Trait Tokens. For a Secret rarity unit like the Dark Swordsman, investing in top-tier traits is highly recommended.

<Callout type="warning" title="Trait Token Management">
**Do not burn Trait Tokens on units you will replace soon.** Save them for your core Mythic and Secret units, especially your main damage dealers like the Dark Swordsman. Rerolling can be expensive, so target the best traits.
</Callout>
