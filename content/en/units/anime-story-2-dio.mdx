<Section icon={Swords} title="Skills and Passive Ability Breakdown">

The true strength of **anime story 2 dio** lies in his unique passives. Unlike many other units that rely on pure stat checks, Dio utilizes time manipulation and stacking mechanics to overwhelm opponents in both PvE and PvP scenarios.

**Muda Chain** is his primary offensive passive. It allows Dio to build up critical chance and critical damage over the course of a battle. As the chain grows, he performs powerful follow-up attacks that can bypass standard defenses. Because this passive naturally scales his crit stats, your equipment focus should shift toward other attributes.

**Stand Mastery** is the definitive "Time Stop" ability. Every three full turns, time freezes for everyone on the battlefield except for the Majestic Vampire. This grants him an immediate extra turn, effectively allowing for double attacks or a chance to reset cooldowns before the enemy can react.

| Passive Name | Effect Description | Trigger Condition |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Muda Chain** | Stacks Crit Chance/Damage and follow-up hits | Continuous attacking |
| **Stand Mastery** | Time freeze and extra turn granted | Every 3 full turns |
| **Vampiric Reach** | Increased range and life-steal potential | Active during night |

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Strategy">
Use Stand Mastery to time your highest-cooldown skills. Since you get an extra turn, you can unload your full combo during the freeze without fear of counter-attacks.
</Callout>
