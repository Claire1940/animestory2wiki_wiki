### Evolution Steps for Yuno

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain Base Yuno">
    Successfully clear **Floor 500** of the Nature Tower. This will grant you a copy of Yuno. The tower resets daily, so you can attempt this repeatedly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Evolution Materials">
    Farm the Nature Tower for the necessary items:
    - **Fairy Spirit**: Found on **Floor 333**.
    - **Secret Orbs**: You'll need **10** of these. They are obtained daily from the tower.
    - **Ascended Orbs**: Also obtained daily from the tower. These are required for Yuno's final Ascended form.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate Evolution">
    Navigate to the "Evolutions" menu in-game. Select Yuno and apply the Fairy Spirit, Secret Orbs, and Ascended Orbs to transform him. It's often recommended to roll for desired stats on Yuno *before* evolving him.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock Asta's Sword (Optional but Recommended)">
    Evolving both Yuno to his Ascended form and Julius will unlock the Asta Sword, which is crucial for evolving Asta, another top-tier unit. This creates a powerful synergy for your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>
