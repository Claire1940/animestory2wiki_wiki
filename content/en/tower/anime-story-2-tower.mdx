### Yuno's Evolution Path

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Base Yuno">
    Successfully clear Floor 500 of the Nature Tower. Yuno has an 80% drop chance, with a 20% chance for a shiny version.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Fairy Spirit">
    The Fairy Spirit is an evolution item for Yuno, found on Floor 333 of the Nature Tower.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Collect Secret Orbs">
    You need 10 Secret Orbs for Yuno's first evolution. These are also obtainable from the Nature Tower.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Obtain Ascended Orbs">
    To reach Yuno's Ascended form, you'll need Ascended Orbs. These are a rare daily drop from the Nature Tower. Each day, you can only get a limited number of these orbs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Evolve Yuno and Julius">
    Evolving both Yuno and Julius to their Ascended forms is part of a questline that rewards the Asta Sword, essential for evolving Asta.
  </Step>
</Steps>
