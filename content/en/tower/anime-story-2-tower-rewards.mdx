<Section icon={Shield} title="Nature Tower Elemental Restrictions">

The Nature Tower introduced a significant shift in the meta by implementing a strict elemental penalty. If you enter the Nature Tower with any unit that does not possess the **Nature** or **Neutral** element, your damage output is reduced to a measly 1%. This makes standard high-DPS units from other elements completely useless in this mode.

To succeed here, you must pivot your strategy toward units like Guts or Sid. These units maintain their full damage potential and often bring utility that bypasses the high HP pools of tower enemies.

| Element | Damage in Nature Tower | Recommended Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Nature** | 100% (Full Damage) | Primary DPS and Support |
| **Neutral** | 100% (Full Damage) | Versatile Carry units |
| **Fire/Water/Other** | 1% (Penalty) | Do not use unless for specific passives |
| **Boss Elements** | Variable | Check floor details before entering |

The current king of the Nature Tower is undoubtedly Guts. His status as a Neutral/Dark unit allows him to bypass the heavy penalties while applying a devastating Bleed DoT (Damage over Time) that scales with his attack stats.

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Always check your unit's element before starting a Nature Tower run. Even a Triple-S tier unit will fail if they are hitting for only 1% of their intended damage.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Tower Preparation">

Before attempting to climb the leaderboard for the best rewards, you must ensure your team is properly equipped. High-level play in Anime Story 2 requires more than just high-rarity units; it requires "Monarch" tier stats and perfect traits.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Summon and Ascend">
    Focus your Gems on banners featuring Guts or Aizen. Once obtained, use duplicates or ascension materials to reach Level 300. Ascension is mandatory to survive the damage scaling after Floor 10.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Reroll for Monarch">
    The Monarch trait is a game-changer. It provides massive boosts to HP and DPS. Aim for Triple S or Double S stats in Attack and Speed to ensure your units move first in the turn order.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Equip Synergy Units">
    Don't just fill your slots with DPS. Ensure you have at least one CC unit (Aizen/Julius) and one survival unit (Yami/Julius). Balance is key to overcoming high-floor bosses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Analyze Floor Elements">
    Before clicking 'Start', check the floor's elemental affinity. Swap in Asta if the enemies use stuns, or bring extra Nature units for the Nature Tower.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Optimization Tip">
If you are stuck on a floor with Itachi or Asta, remember that Asta is immune to CC. You cannot stun him, so you must rely on Yami's immunity or Guts's dodge to survive his initial onslaught.
</Callout>
