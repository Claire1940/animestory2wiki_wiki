<Section icon={Map} title="Mastering the New Nature Tower Game Mode">

The **Nature Tower** is a significant addition in the **Anime Story 2 Black Clover Update**, offering daily resets and strategic elemental challenges. This mode is crucial for obtaining new units, evolving existing ones, and acquiring valuable rerolls and shiny units.

**How to Conquer the Nature Tower:**

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Understand Elemental Buffs">
    In Nature Tower, **Nature** and **Fire** units receive a significant double damage buff. Conversely, neutral, nature, and non-fire unit passives are disabled. Focus your team composition around these elements.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Assemble a Meta Team">
    Prioritize Nature and Fire units. Units like **Yuno** and **Broly** are highly effective due to their innate elemental advantages and powerful abilities. Consider tanks like Yami for survivability.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize Leader Skills">
    The new Leader Skill system is vital here. Place a unit with a beneficial leader skill in slot one to maximize team potential. For example, Asta's buff for sword users can be powerful if you have strong sword units who are also Fire or Nature.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Strategic Placement">
    Position your damage dealers to benefit from buffs and your tanks to absorb incoming damage. The invulnerability of units like Yami can buy crucial turns.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Daily Resets & Rewards">
    The Nature Tower resets daily, so make it a part of your routine. Rewards include rerolls and a chance to get shiny units, which possess insane stats.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Nature Tower Meta Units">
For optimal performance in the Nature Tower, prioritize units such as **Yuno**, **Broly**, **Genos**, and **Escanor**. These units benefit immensely from the elemental buffs, allowing them to deal incredibly high damage and carry your team through the tower.
</Callout>
