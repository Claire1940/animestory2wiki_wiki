Successfully navigating the Rimuru Portal requires a clear plan for each wave. The difficulty scales, with the third wave being the most challenging. Follow these steps to ensure a smooth solo clear.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unit Placement and Initial Setup">
    Before the first wave begins, place your Level 300 Broly strategically to cover as much of the map as possible. Ensure he can hit multiple enemies with his abilities. If you have any support units, place them to maximize their buffs or debuffs on incoming enemies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Managing Wave 1 & 2">
    The first two waves are generally straightforward with a strong Broly. Focus on observing enemy spawn patterns and adjusting your unit's targeting if necessary. Utilize any active abilities your units have to clear groups quickly. These waves should be relatively easy to pass.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Conquering Wave 3 (Slime Demon)">
    Wave 3 introduces the main boss, the Slime Demon, and often a higher density of stronger regular enemies. This is where your Broly's full power is needed. Activate all available buffs and offensive abilities. Focus fire on the Slime Demon while Broly's area damage clears the smaller threats. Constant damage output is key to defeating the boss within the time limit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Post-Clear Farming">
    Once the Rimuru Portal is cleared, you'll receive Slime Tokens and other event rewards. Repeat the process to efficiently farm these tokens. The faster you can clear, the more runs you can complete within the event window. Consider setting up an auto-clicker if allowed by game rules for extended farming sessions.
  </Step>
</Steps>
