  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Gold District (World 6) - Yami">
    The Gold District, usually World 6, houses a boss that drops the highly coveted **Yami** unit. This boss is often situated in an elevated or secluded part of the map, requiring a bit more exploration. Yami units typically have powerful leader skills that can significantly boost captain allies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Last Map (World 7) - Black Bull">
    The final secret boss is located in the game's last map, often referred to as World 7 or the "final kingdom." This boss drops the **Black Bull** unit. Given its location, this boss is usually the most challenging. The Black Bull unit, like Yami, often provides significant buffs to captain allies, making it a cornerstone for certain team compositions.
